Frequently asked questions



What's in it for me?

Monthly commissions paid directly to you, sales incentives and associate-type merchandise discounts as offered by Stuart Weitzman, traffic you can access from the website of Stuart Weitzman.



What do I have to pay to join the program?

Nothing, the program is completely free. Plus, you don't have to deal with webstore design or development. On top of that, you don't have to collect money from customers or manage the returns.



Do you offer any training?

A seasoned Salesfoor trainer will help you getting up and running and help you with best practices.



Do I work for Salesfloor or for Stuart Weitzman?

You represent Stuart Weitzman and work for yourself. Salesfloor is here to provide you with the platform, the brand relationship, the support and ongoing advice on how to maximize the opportunity.



How much do I have to sell?

It is up to you but the more you sell the more incentives and benefits there are.
