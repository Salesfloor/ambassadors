How it works



Get Started

Once you are selected, you’ll receive a personalized web store within minutes. The web store - or the store front as we call it - is your very own branded and personalized version of your favourite brand’s website.


Salesfloor will train you on how to simply and easily curate the products you believe, in addition to showing you how to personalize the platform and how to reach out to your network right away.


You’ll also have access to branded content, promotional recommendations and shopping services to amplify the selling experience.



Do your magic

Once set up, you’ll connect and professionally market yourself to customers on-line and transact on your store front whenever you want, wherever you want.


You’ll be able to access your great network and even earn commissions serving the millions of customers shopping on Stuart Weitzman website, making it one of the most powerful sales drivers for any type of ambassador program out there!



Enjoy the ride

Earn generous commissions on all product sales and enjoy employee discounts on your own product purchases as well as other sales incentives.


No worries about building your own site - it’s done!


Payment processing, shipping and inventory management are all taken care of by Stuart Weitzman so all you have to do is do what you do best - curate and advise people on the best products and make sales commissions online through your personal site.

Frequently asked questions



What's in it for me?

Monthly commissions paid directly to you, sales incentives and associate-type merchandise discounts as offered by Stuart Weitzman, traffic you can access from the website of Stuart Weitzman.



What do I have to pay to join the program?

Nothing, the program is completely free. Plus, you don't have to deal with webstore design or development. On top of that, you don't have to collect money from customers or manage the returns.



Do you offer any training?

A seasoned Salesfoor trainer will help you getting up and running and help you with best practices.



Do I work for Salesfloor or for Stuart Weitzman?

You represent Stuart Weitzman and work for yourself. Salesfloor is here to provide you with the platform, the brand relationship, the support and ongoing advice on how to maximize the opportunity.



How much do I have to sell?

It is up to you but the more you sell the more incentives and benefits there are.
