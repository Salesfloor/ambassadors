How it works

Get Started

Once you are matched, you’ll receive a personalized web store within a popular retail brands’ website, within minutes. The web store - or the store front as we call it - is your very own branded and personalized version of your favourite brand’s website.

Salesfloor will train you on how to simply and easily curate the products you believe in from the retail brands’ site, in addition to showing you how to personalize the platform and how to reach out to your network right away.

You’ll also have access to branded content, promotional recommendations and shopping services to amplify the selling experience.

Do your magic

Once set up, you’ll connect and professionally market yourself to customers on-line and transact on your store front whenever you want, wherever you want.

You’ll be able to access your great network and even earn commissions serving the millions of customers shopping on the retailer’s site, making it one of the most powerful sales drivers for any type of ambassador program out there!

Enjoy the ride

Earn generous commissions on all product sales and enjoy employee discounts on your own product purchases as well as other sales incentives.

No worries about building your own site - it’s done!

Payment processing, shipping and inventory management are all taken care of by the retailer so all you have to do is do what you do best - curate and advise people on the best products and make sales commissions on-line through your personal site.

FAQ

Do I work for Salesfloor or for the Brand?

You represent the brand and work for yourself. Salesfloor is here to provide you with the platform, the brand relationship, the support and ongoing advice on how to maximize the opportunity.

How much do I have to sell?

It is up to you but the more you sell the more incentives and benefits there are and the greater access you have to new brands.

Can I represent more than one brand?

Depending on your profile, you can represent more than one brand. That said, most new A-List Ambassadors will start with one and gradually have the opportunity to take on others once a strong sales track record is achieved.

Do I need experience to become an A-List Ambassador?

Sales experience and a knowledge of the industry (i.e fashion) and the brand you want to represent are important assets. That said, if you are passionate and driven, have a great network and want to succeed there is a good chance you will qualify and have an illustrious career as an A-List Ambassador!

How do I earn sales commissions?

You earn commission whenever anyone (a client, a non-client, or yourself) purchases from your Salesfloor store front. In addition to sales commissions and incentives, Salesfloor rewards top sellers with exclusive shopping perks, bonuses and discounts.

How do I track my sales?

You can access a detailed Earnings report in your Salesfloor Backoffice. Please note that it may take a few days for new sales to be visible, and bonuses and incentives are not included in reporting.

How do I get paid?

Salesfloor pays you with PayPal according to a recurring payment schedule. You can use PayPal to easily deposit your commissions to your bank account.

What happens to my application if there is no match for a retail brand currently in the Salesfloor roster?

Salesfloor pays you with PayPal according to a recurring payment schedule. You can use PayPal to easily deposit your commissions to your bank account.

What do I get after I am approved?

You will get your own customizable website and URL, plus training on how to start marketing to your customers. You will also have full access to your retailer’s product catalogue and marketing resources. You can cancel at any point.

How do I get clients?

You are an entrepreneur. You grow your own clientele by reaching out to your social networks in person and via email, and by marketing yourself through social media. Salesfloor also helps connect you to new leads from the retailer’s website with live chat and shopping service apps.

Do clients pay more when they shop with me?

No, your clients buy from the retailer’s website and they are charged only for the products they buy. Your commissions are not included in any of your clients’ transaction information.

What happens when a client makes a return?

Since the item was returned, it will be removed from your monthly sales amount and will not count towards your total commission.

Where can I find training/marketing support?

Salesfloor offers customized selling and marketing tools for ambassadors. For more information about training, please contact support@salesfloor.net

If my client is having an issue with her order, who do I contact?

Email support@salesfloor.net with the details. Please include the client’s full name, email address and transaction ID.

Can I bring my customers in-store to buy?

You can bring your customers in-store to shop and browse, however sales must be made through your web store for you to receive your commissions. If in-store with a customer, we suggest processing the purchase in your web store using your mobile or tablet.

Am I employed by the retailer?

No, you are an independent contractor representing the retailer. You are not an employee.
